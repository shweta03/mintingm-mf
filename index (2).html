Why the overlay worked in 2008: The Dec 2007 annual rebalance detected Sensex/Gold = 12.4x > 9x threshold. The rule automatically shifted 70% of the equity bucket to gold. When Nifty fell 58% in 2008, gold rose 25% — the portfolio barely moved. At Dec 2008 rebalance, ratio had fallen to 6.8x < 9x — overlay switched back to 70% equity, automatically buying equities at market bottom. This is systematic buy-low, sell-high with no human emotion involved.
